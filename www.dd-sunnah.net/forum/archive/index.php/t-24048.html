Turkey, Another Son of Esau


Sermon by Wayne Bedwell

March 8, 2003


Several years ago I gave a sermon entitled The "Palestinians." In it we described that they are descendants of Esau or Edom. We described where they came from, what their attitudes are toward Israel, and what their future will be. It is on our website for those of you who are interested in reading it or hearing it by audio tape.

But Esau (Edom) has other descendants beside the so-called Palestinians. One of them is very much in todayТs news. Those other descendants include the people of Turkey.


Today I want to talk about Turkey; how we know the Turks are descended from Esau, son of Isaac, and what prophesy tells us about them.


First of all letТs prove that Esau and Edom are the same person. LetТs turn to Gen. 36 for that.

Gen 36:8 (NKJV) So Esau dwelt in Mount Seir. Esau <is> Edom.
