The Benevolence of Islam

Author: Salim Al-Hilali

edition:2006 The Prophet said: "Allah loves the one who is lenient and kind in all matters". This book presents Islam as a way of life based on divine teachings such as kindeness, justice, and charity only to mention a few. The Halal and Haram aim at making people lead a happy life by forbidding vice and commanding good. The author tells us that the eminent, benevolent and kind Muslims are as one firm construction. They help one another to achieve good in this world and the Hereafter.
