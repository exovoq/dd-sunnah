An apology is a serious thing, and the words "I'm sorry" should be used with sincerity


So, of what does an apology consist? To offer a sincere apology requires four things:



Admitting what you did wrong

Explaining why you did it

Expressing regret that it happened

Promising not to do it again

=====
Definitions:

1. apologetic: feeling or expressing regret for an action that has upset or inconvenienced somebody, or is likely to do so


2. sympathetic: feeling or expressing sympathy or empathy, especially because of something that has happened
I felt sorry it had to end that way.
Don't start feeling sorry for yourself.

====
An apology is not complete if it does not reflect all four of these:

regret,
understanding of the problem,
acceptance of responsibility, and
willingness to do better
