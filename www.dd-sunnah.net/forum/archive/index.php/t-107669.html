The Universality of Islam

Author: Abdullah H Al-Kahtany


Edition:1978
In a world that is changing very rapidly, many
tend to forget about religion, believing it to be
cosmetic rather than essential. They are running,
trying to get so-called happiness. But, sooner or later,
they find themselves chasing a mere mirage.
This book tries to direct those who have
gone astray to the right path: the path of Islam. It
disproves the claims that Islam is neither valid in this
era nor capable of providing effective remedies to
universal problems. For, in addition to being the
ultimate religion until the coming of the hereafter, it is
universal.
