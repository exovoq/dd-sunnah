As Salaamu Alaikum ; Peace be Upon you,


Narrated Abu Huraira (RA):

I heard Allah's Apostle (SAW) saying, Allah divided Mercy into one-hundred parts and He kept its ninety-nine parts with Him and sent down its one part on the earth, and because of that, its one single part, His creations are Merciful to each other, so that even the mare lifts up its hoofs away from its baby animal, lest it should trample on it."


Source: Sahih Bukhari, Book 73, Number 29.

Mare: is a fully mature female horse or other equine animal.

Hoofs: the entire foot of a horse, donkey, etc.
